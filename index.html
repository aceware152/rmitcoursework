The Project

My project details, Sep 10, 2019

Scope

A tomahawk guided missile hits its target by constantly checking its heading and adjusting course to compensate for accuracy losses, hitting targets with deadly accuracy from 1,600km away.

The scope of this project is to create an advertising application with the same accuracy as a tomahawk. Acquiring customers autonomously and with pinpoint accuracy. Interfacing with various API’s such as google adds and Facebook adds, its primary mission will be to use intuitive algorithms to beat the market and reduce the cost per conversion for a wide variety of customers and industries. Make no mistake advertising is a war and we need smart weapons.

This application will be of great benefit to anyone using it, as it will put them in head of their competition and will generate sales that they could have only dreamed of achieving prior to using this application.

Facebook alone generated $16.6 billion in ad revenue for the second quarter of 2019. This is a huge market and anyone looking to get the edge on their competitors will be very interested in this application as a product to aid their business. The value of this product is in the value it returns in sales to its customers.

Main features

Learning

Learning is continual and will require a small amount of finances to continually take samples that it can use to build strategy from. For example, the application finds that the best target group on Sunday morning a week before Christmas is young men with gift suggestions this does not mean that this is your target market for this product or service. If over a period of a year that this result was consistent then it would be almost certain that the best time to target men of that age with gift suggestions is Sunday morning. To have any degree of certainty about anything the application would need to take samples over an extended period, perhaps years.

Once the AI is fully trained will have an advanced tactical advantage that it can use to generate sales for the customers of the application. This will generate a large financial gain for the owners of this software. As the data is collected the application grows and evolves its algorithm to only target the right groups of people at the right time with the right products. This will generate higher results for advertising dollars for the customers of this product.

Laser targeted advertising.

When a target has been identified by the application it strikes with laser precision. Using the data collected it selects the advertisement, the groups of people, their interests and the time of day that the advertisement will be active. This is all about people hacking, there are times of day / week / year when people are more likely to spend any and all information legally obtainable will be gathered.

The more information that can be legally gathered and legally used to accurately target audiences the better. Example the application knows that a birthday for a person in a household is 4 weeks away it selectively targets the people who are close to that person with gift suggestions from the companies that employ our application. This will be achieved with customer discovery protocols all information that is available publicly and is legally able to be used will be employed in the targeting algorithms. Using this approach will generate more revenue.

Reporting

Reports will be automatically generated and in a human readable language. The AI will speak like a person. Example: “I have decided to include people looking for a home loan in furniture advertising campaigns because our sampling indicates that people who have recently visited sites for home loans have been entering our funnels for furniture sales. Reporting will have many features including data analytics, bar graphs, push messaging and will be fully customisable for a small fee. By allowing customers of the product to be shown only the information they are interested in when they are interested in seeing it. As advances in AI and voice assistance are becoming increasingly intelligent, this system could eventually have a personal assistant that could be spoken to in human language and could give verbal reports of what is being achieved with the application.

Tools and Technologies

For the seed AI to be initialised it would need very little more than a dedicated computer with good web access. A high capacity, High speed hard drive with a massive database. As the seed begins to develop it will grow in a similar way to how google grew in the early days at Stanford, eventually the university resources were being drained to such an extent that they were asked to leave or shut the project down. Any system that is partly self directed and continually collecting data will eventually grow larger than its current container. The programming software requirements are reasonably flexible as the API’s have SDK’s in a variety of languages.

Skill Required

Reasonably advanced skill in one or more computer languages would be required to begin this task. Proficiencies in database design and a reasonable understanding of AI would also be of great advantage. The skill would be hard to find at all to develop this product into a high value application. The necessary finances for the hires required would be beyond most university students budget. Perhaps an open source development on git hub with contributors earning a stake in the final product could be possible. Access to the live version and the data collected would need to be secure as this is where the intrinsic value of the application lies.

Outcome

When this project becomes successful it will generate great value to all stake holders, customer acquisition market would be changed significantly for all companies that employed this technology. The landscape of marketing would be forever changed and the power once only available to multi million dollar corporations could be in the hands of small start-ups desperately trying to find the market for their product or service.
